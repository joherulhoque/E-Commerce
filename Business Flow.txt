📦 1. Product Setup (Initial Setup)

👉 দোকান শুরু করার আগে:

Product category তৈরি (Food, Grocery, Cosmetics)
Product entry (Name, Price, Barcode)
Unit setup (kg, pcs, liter)
Rice - 1kg - 80 Tk
Oil - 1 liter - 160 Tk
Soap - 1 pcs - 25 Tk
🛒 2. Purchase (Supplier থেকে কেনা)

👉 দোকান মালিক supplier থেকে পণ্য আনে

Supplier selection
Purchase order
Goods receive
Stock entry update
Supplier → Goods → Invoice → Stock increase

✔ Example:

100 kg rice purchase
Stock +100 update
📦 3. Stock Management

👉 সব পণ্য stock এ জমা থাকে

Available stock check
Low stock alert
Damage/expired item remove
Stock = Opening + Purchase - Sales
🧾 4. Sales (POS System)

👉 Customer দোকানে এসে কিনে

Product select
Barcode scan / manual add
Cart add
Discount apply
Bill generate
Product → Cart → Total → Payment → Invoice
💰 5. Payment

👉 Customer payment দেয়

Cash
Card
Mobile banking (bKash, Nagad)

✔ After payment:

Invoice print
Stock decrease
🧾 6. Invoice / Receipt

👉 Customer কে bill দেওয়া হয়

Invoice number
Product list
Total amount
Date/time
📊 7. Daily Sales Report

👉 দিনের শেষে হিসাব:

Total sales
Profit
Loss
Top selling product
Sales Report = Total Income - Cost
📦 8. Stock Update Automatically

👉 Sales হলে stock কমে যায়

Stock = Stock - Sold Quantity
👤 9. Customer Management (Optional)
Customer name
Due balance
Purchase history
💸 10. Profit Calculation
